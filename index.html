How to Play

Every round the Wizard will give you a key word. You will have 20 seconds to input all the adjective, nouns, etc. that you think of when you read the key word. Make sure to think outside the box! For example, if you had the word Reptile, you could type out any one of these words: extinct, scaly, gecko, tiny, tropical, dinosaur. take this text out

Type out the word in the Wizz Out! section and hit ENTER. To make sure the Wizard can understand your word, have the correct spelling. If your word matches our Wizard's word list you get a point! Accumulate as many points as you can and find out if you can out wizz our Wizard!
