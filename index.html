How to Play

Every round Mr Wizard will give you a key word. You have 20 seconds to input all the adjective, nouns, etc. that you think of related to that word. HINT! Think outside the box! For example: 'Reptile' could type these words: extinct, scaly, gecko, tiny, tropical, dinosaur.

Type out the word in the Wiz Out! section and hit ENTER. To make sure the Wizard can understand your word, have the correct spelling. If your word matches our Wizard's word list you get a point! Accumulate as many points as you can and find out if you can out-wiz our Wizard!
